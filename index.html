Product Brief Description

After the COVID-19 pandemic, the world becomes unstable . World governments initiated new defined laws to reduce the spread of the virus. One of the major imposed laws are individuals wearing masks and maintaining social distance. This law became the must in every country and the only hope to prevent the disease for now. The major problem is how to detect violators automatically and in real time. To fill the gap , we are in . We, a group of passionate engineers, came around, planned and designed a AI framework which can detect , analyze and alert the people regarding their mask and social distance violations. We used deep learning and computer vision techniques to develop an ensemble algorithm which can do the job by its cognitive capabilities . All we want is , to make the world a better place to live in this pandemic.
